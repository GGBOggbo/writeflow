# Cool Editorial HyperFrames Design

## Style Prompt

Create a calm editorial control room for focused content work. The composition should feel clear, awake, and strategic rather than cozy or sleepy. Motion should feel deliberate and refined: measured rises, crisp card reveals, and quiet progress emphasis instead of flashy kinetic effects.

## Colors

- `#F3F5F7` canvas background
- `#233044` primary ink
- `#5F7993` cool accent
- `#6C8B74` strategic olive
- `#FFFFFF` elevated surface

## Typography

- Sans: `Inter`, `system-ui`, `sans-serif`
- Mono/label accent: `IBM Plex Mono`, `ui-monospace`, `monospace`

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon, cyberpunk, or generic SaaS blues.
- Do not use sleepy beige or paper-yellow washes.
- Do not use bouncy or playful motion.
- Do not overload the frame with icons or tiny labels.
- Do not make it look like a developer admin panel.
